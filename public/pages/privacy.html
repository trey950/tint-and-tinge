Privacy Policy

Effective June 18, 2026 · Last updated June 18, 2026

Tint and Tinge ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you use our website at tintandtinge.com (the "Site") and our personal color analysis service (the "Service").

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Name and email address. Collected at checkout so we can deliver your analysis and provide customer support.
  • Questionnaire answers. Your responses about skin tone, vein color, hair, eyes, and other features. We use these to compute your color season.
  • Payment information. Card details are entered on Stripe's hosted checkout page and never touch our servers. We receive a transaction confirmation from Stripe — no card numbers.

Information we do not collect

  • Photographs. If you upload photos to personalize the analysis experience, they are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. They are never uploaded to our servers, never stored, and never transmitted anywhere.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data. Basic analytics (page views, browser type, referring URL) via Meta Pixel and our server logs, for advertising attribution and to improve the Service.
  • Cookies. We use cookies for session management and to enable the Meta Pixel. You can disable cookies in your browser; the Service will still function.

2. How we use your information

  • To deliver your color analysis by email.
  • To process your payment and prevent fraud (via Stripe).
  • To respond to support inquiries.
  • To send you optional follow-up emails with related product recommendations and affiliate offers (you can opt out at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email).
  • To measure advertising effectiveness via the Meta Pixel.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who help us operate the Service:

  • Stripe for payment processing (stripe.com/privacy).
  • Resend for transactional email delivery (resend.com/legal/privacy-policy).
  • Render for hosting (render.com/privacy).
  • Meta for advertising attribution and retargeting (facebook.com/privacy/policy).

We may also disclose information when required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Tint and Tinge, our users, or others.

4. Data retention

We retain your order records (name, email, season, transaction ID) for as long as required by applicable tax, accounting, and consumer protection laws — typically 7 years. You can request deletion of your account data at any time by emailing hello@tintandtinge.com; we will delete what we can while retaining only what the law requires.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, and to opt out of certain processing. California residents have rights under the CCPA; EU/UK residents have rights under the GDPR. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@tintandtinge.com.

6. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. International transfers

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access it from elsewhere, your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted at the top of the page and, for active customers, communicated by email.

9. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints? Email us at hello@tintandtinge.com.
